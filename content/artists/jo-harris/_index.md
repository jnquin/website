Jo Harris performs classical repertoire bridging Bach to Bingham as a soloist. Endorsed by Help Musicians, who currently support her as an artist, Jo has released solo and chamber works under the Endelium and Delphian labels. 

Jo has also performed for specialist ensembles Gabrieli, Orchestre Révolutionnaire et Romantique and for Welsh National Opera. She plays regularly with an exciting new orchestra called 'Alina' and for the Royal Opera House's educational department.

Her studio experience includes recordings with Britten Sinfonia for Chandos and Hyperion, live concert broadcasts with Bella Tromba for BBC Radio 3 and Classic FM and soundtracks for Universal Studios.
