Bristol-based conductor Elinor Cooper studied at the University of Bristol and Royal Welsh College of Music. She is currently Musical Director of Bristol University Singers, Collegium Singers, and Swindon Choral Society, and Assistant Director of the prestigious Rodolfus Choir, working closely with Artistic Director Ralph Allwood. Elinor also works with the National Youth Choirs of Great Britain, is a member of professional choir Celestia, and regularly appears with professional choirs throughout the UK.
