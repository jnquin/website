Joanna Tomlinson has established an outstanding reputation for her work as conductor, singer and vocal coach with choirs of all ages and sizes. She is Artistic Director of the Military Wives Choirs, and conductor of Constanza Chorus and Whitehall Choir in London. Joanna has conducted at venues including the Royal Albert Hall, Cadogan Hall and St John's Smith Square.
