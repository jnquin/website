Jason is currently the Head of Music at a London Secondary School, Musical Director of the Landmark Singers and Lantern Youth Choir, alongside regular work with National Youth Choir.

Born in Durham and based in London, Jason is a music graduate of Royal Holloway where he achieved a First-Class Honours degree and started the octet ‘Voce Nova’.
