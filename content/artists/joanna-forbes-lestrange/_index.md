Joanna Forbes L’Estrange is a celebrated British vocalist, composer and conductor known for her versatile artistry. A descendant of an eminent musical family, she earned an MA in Music from Oxford University and began her career with seven years at the helm of the Grammy® Award-winning vocal group The Swingle Singers.
