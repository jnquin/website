Ben has over 30 years' experience as a conductor, composer, producer, singer and arranger. He is Director of the professional choir London Voices. He has composed many works for choirs and singers, and his music has been performed all over the world. He has worked with many major choirs and orchestras both in the UK and abroad, as well as in the recording studio for film, games and TV soundtracks.
