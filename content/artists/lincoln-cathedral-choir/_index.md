Lincoln Cathedral Choir sings every day, except Wednesday, during term-time. The Cathedral Choir is a professional choir made up of 40 boys and girls, six lay vicars and five choral scholars also sometimes joined by junior choral scholars. The choir is led by Director of Music and Master of the Choristers, Aric Prentice, and by the Cathedral Organist, Jeffrey Makinson.
