Lucy Russell is an internationally regarded violinist, quartet leader and teacher. She is among the most distinguished of international violinists who have achieved eminence on both historical instruments and their ‘modern’ counterparts, performing and recording music from Monteverdi to the present day with equal distinction and authority. She is Professor of Baroque Violin at the Royal College of Music, Honorary Professor of Strings at the University of St Andrews, and Baroque Violin Tutor at the Royal Birmingham Conservatoire.
